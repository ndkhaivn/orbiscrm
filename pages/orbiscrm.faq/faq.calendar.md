                    How do I create a new event (more quickly) by dragging?
                </a>
            </h4>
        </div>
        <div id="how-do-i-add-a-new-event-more-quickly-by-dragging" class="panel-collapse collapse noCrossRef">
            <div class="panel-body">
                <ol>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">Click the button to change to【Weekly】view</li>
                    <img src="images/calendar/calendar-week.png" alt="calendar-week.png" style="width: 80%"><br>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">
                        Click and <span style="color: red">Drag</span> the【Period】of the event 
                        <span style="font-style: italic; font-weight: normal">(E.g. 17:00 to 19:00 on 06/02/2020)</span>
                    </li>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">The popup window contains a pre-filled【Title】
                        <span style="font-weight: normal"> [Meeting with]</span>
                    </li>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">Confirm the【Start & End Time】</li>
                    <img src="images/calendar/calendar-click-drag-period.png" alt="calendar-click-drag-period.png" style="width: 70%"><br>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">Finalise the【Title】with client name 
                        <span style="font-style: italic; font-weight: normal">(E.g. Meeting with John Bush)</span>
                    </li>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">Fill in the【Description】
                        <span style="font-style: italic; font-weight: normal">(E.g. Confirm the meeting with John)</span>
                    </li>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">Type an email address to send an【Invite Email】
                        <span style="font-style: italic; font-weight: normal">(E.g. johnbush@mailinator.com)</span>
                    </li>
                    <img src="images/calendar/calendar-new-event.png" alt="calendar-new-event.png" style="width: 50%"><br>
                    <li style="font-weight: bold">Finally, click the【Save Event】button</li>
                    <img src="images/calendar/calendar-save-event.png" alt="calendar-save-event.png" style="width: 50%"><br>
                </ol>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
